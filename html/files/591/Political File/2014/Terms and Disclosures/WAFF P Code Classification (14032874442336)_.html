Non-Preemptible 1 - Spots scheduled to air at WAFF-TV’s discretion within or adjacent to the particular program, time period, daypart, or day as specified by the advertiser. These spots may not be preempted in favor of any other advertisement and will air as scheduled, in the absence of unforeseen circumstances, including, but not limited to, program changes, technical difficulties, breaking news, and human error. Because WAFF-TV has a limited number of commercial avails, even "non-preemptible" time may be sold out. In this case, WAFF-TV will endeavor to locate a comparable area for placement of a candidate's time. If a spot in this class of time is preempted, WAFF-TV will endeavor to provide the advertiser with a make-good, as provided below. Preemptible with Notice 2 - Spots scheduled to air at WAFF-TV’s discretion within or adjacent to the particular program, time period, daypart, or day as specified by the advertiser. These spots may be preempted in favor of other advertisements in a higher class of time upon 5 days notice to the advertiser. If a spot in this class of time is preempted, WAFF-TV will endeavor to provide the advertiser with a make-good, as provided below. These spots must be purchased more than 72 hours before they are to air. Preemptible with Notice 3 - Spots scheduled to air at WAFF-TV’s discretion within or adjacent to the particular program, time period, daypart, or day as specified by the advertiser. These spots may be preempted in favor of other advertisements in a higher class of time upon 10 day notice to the advertiser. If a spot in this class of time is preempted, WAFF-TV will endeavor to provide the advertiser with a make-good, as provided below. These spots must be purchased more than 24 hours before they are to air. Immediately Preemptible with No Notice 4 - Spots scheduled to air at WAFF-TV’s discretion within or adjacent to the particular program, time period, daypart, or day as specified by the advertiser. These spots may be preempted in favor of other advertisements in a higher class of time, or in the event of unforeseen program changes, technical difficulties, breaking news, human error or similar circumstances with no guarantee of advance notice to the advertiser. If a spot in this class of time is preempted, WAFF-TV will endeavor to provide the advertiser with a make-good, as provided below.
